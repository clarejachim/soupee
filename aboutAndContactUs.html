Our Story: We are Soupeé

Soupeé was born in a humble kitchen in Indiana and has had the opportunity to spread homemade meals filled with nutrition and love for everyone to experience for themselves.

Each and every recipe that we have on our menu has come from my grandparent’s cookbook or when I would help my parents in the kitchen. My favorite memories have been those of cooking a meal for those I love!

Growing up and moving out of my childhood home, I wanted to find some way to connect with what made me feel good inside. What better way than to eat a bowl of soup! Thus, Soupeé was created to share the love and warmth from my kitchen to you.
