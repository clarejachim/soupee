About Us

Soupeé is a homestyle restaurant where we are focused on bringing love from the kitchen to your soup bowl. We take pride in using bone broth to elevate our soups in nutrition and flavor. Let us take care of you on your lunch break or whenever you need a bite to eat.


Soupeé was born in a humble kitchen in Indiana over 15 years ago. Every recipe on our menu has come from my grandparent’s cookbook or from evenings in my parents in the kitchen.

Each and every recipe that we have on our menu has come from my grandparent’s cookbook or when I would help my parents in the kitchen. My favorite memories have been of cooking a meal for those I love!

Mission

Healthy Soup for every season, focussed on bone broth.

Vision

Life is full of deliciousness!

Values

Delicious soup connecting people with food they love, with care.
